About

Hidaya Tul Quran

Are you looking for a reliable online Quran teaching academy that fits your busy schedule? Hidaya-tul-Quran Academy bridges the gap between students and qualified scholars. Whether you want to learn how to read the Quran with Tajweed from scratch or need a Quran teacher at home via Zoom, we are here to help.

We specialize in providing personalized Islamic education for kids and adults. Our expert tutors ensure that students from the USA, UK, Canada, and UAE can learn to recite the Holy Quran with proper pronunciation (Tajweed) without leaving their homes.

Why choose Hidaya-tul-Quran Academy?
  • Qualified & Experienced Teachers: Our teachers are Huffaz, Tajweed-certified, and well-trained, with experience teaching children and adults worldwide.
  • One-to-One Live Online Classes: We provide personalized 1-on-1 sessions, so every student gets full attention and learns at their own pace.
  • Tajweed-Focused Learning: We emphasize correct pronunciation (Makharij) and proper Tajweed rules, not just reading.
  • Courses for All Ages:
    • Kids (beginners & school-age)
    • Adults (beginners & advanced)
    • New Muslims
    • Everyone is welcome
  • Flexible Class Timings: We offer flexible schedules suitable for USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Europe, and Middle East time zones.
  • Free Trial Class: Students can attend a free trial class before enrolling — no obligation.
  • Structured & Easy Curriculum: Our courses are step-by-step, easy to follow, and designed to build confidence quickly.
  • Progress Tracking & Feedback: Regular assessments, feedback, and progress reports help students improve consistently.
