About

Online Quran Academy

Hidaya Tul Quran

Hidaya-tul-Quran Academy is a trusted online Quran academy providing professional Quran learning services for students worldwide. If you are searching for a Quran teacher online or want to learn Quran with Tajweed from the comfort of your home, we offer structured and personalized learning programs for all ages.

Our academy connects students with experienced and qualified Quran tutors through live online classes. Whether you are a beginner or want to improve your recitation, we help you achieve fluency in reading the Holy Quran with proper pronunciation and Tajweed rules.


Why Choose Our Online Quran Academy?

  • Certified Quran Teachers: Our tutors are highly qualified, Hafiz-e-Quran, and Tajweed-certified with years of teaching experience.
  • One-to-One Online Quran Classes: Personalized sessions ensure better understanding and faster learning for each student.
  • Learn Quran with Tajweed: We focus on correct pronunciation (Makharij) and proper Tajweed rules to improve recitation.
  • Courses for Kids and Adults:
    • Quran reading for beginners
    • Quran recitation with Tajweed
    • Islamic studies for kids
    • Courses for new Muslims
  • Flexible Timings: Classes are available according to USA, UK, Canada, UAE, and worldwide time zones.
  • Free Trial Class: Join a free trial session and experience our teaching quality before enrollment.
  • Progress Tracking: Regular assessments and feedback ensure continuous improvement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make Quran learning online accessible, easy, and effective for students around the world. We aim to provide high-quality Islamic education that helps individuals understand and recite the Holy Quran correctly with Tajweed.
