Online Quran Reading Course

Online Quran Reading Course — From Letters to Full Verses

Our online Quran reading course is designed for students who want to improve their Quran reading from basic words to complete verses. It focuses on fluency, correct pronunciation (Tajweed), and confident recitation so learners can read the Quran smoothly with a deep connection to the words of Allah.

All classes are live, 1-on-1 sessions with a certified tutor. Available for students in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, and worldwide. Free trial class available — start today with no commitment.

Book Free Trial Class

What You Will Learn

  • Fluent Quran reading from letters to full verses
  • Correct pronunciation (Makharij) of all Arabic letters
  • Basic Tajweed rules applied during reading
  • Wazu (ablution) — method and rulings
  • Seerat Nabi (S.A.W.) and multiple Duas
  • Selected Hadith and Islamic etiquette

Who Is This Course For?

  • Students who have completed Noorani Qaida
  • Adults who can read but want to improve fluency
  • Kids learning to read the full Quran
  • Anyone who wants to read Quran with proper Tajweed
  • Students in USA, UK, Canada, Australia and worldwide
