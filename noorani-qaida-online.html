Noorani Qaida Online Course

Learn Noorani Qaida Online — Beginner Quran Foundation Course

Start your Quran journey with our online Noorani Qaida course — the perfect foundation for kids and adults who want to learn how to read the Quran from scratch. Our certified tutors teach Arabic letter recognition, correct pronunciation (Makharij), proper letter joining, and essential Quran reading skills through live 1-on-1 sessions.

Whether you are in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, or anywhere in the world, our flexible online Noorani Qaida classes fit your schedule. Free trial class available — no payment required.

Book Free Trial Class

What You Will Learn

  • Arabic letters — recognition and correct pronunciation
  • Harakaat (vowels) — Fatha, Kasra, Damma and their usage
  • Letter joining rules — how Arabic letters connect in words
  • Tanween, Shadda, Sukoon — essential pronunciation marks
  • Basic Tajweed rules — for correct Quran recitation
  • Six Kalimas, basic Duas, and Hadith
  • Seerat Nabi (S.A.W.) — introduction to Prophet's life

Who Is This Course For?

  • Kids starting Quran from scratch
  • Adults who have never learned to read Arabic
  • New Muslims beginning their Quran journey
  • Anyone wanting a strong foundation before full Quran reading
  • Students in USA, UK, Canada, Australia and worldwide
