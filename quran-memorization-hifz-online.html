Online Quran Memorization (Hifz) Course

Online Hifz Course — Memorize the Quran from Home

Our online Quran memorization (Hifz) course supports students in memorizing short Surahs, selected portions, or the entire Holy Quran. Through a structured and disciplined routine with daily new lessons and regular revision cycles, our certified Hafiz tutors guide you every step of the way.

Live 1-on-1 sessions available for students in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, UAE, and worldwide. Flexible timings for all time zones. Free trial class — no commitment required.

Book Free Trial Class

What You Will Learn

  • Daily new sabaq (new lesson) with memorization techniques
  • Sabqi — revision of recent memorization
  • Manzil — revision of older memorized portions
  • Tajweed during memorization for correct recitation
  • Method of Salah — rulings and translation
  • Wazu and Ghusal — rulings and method
  • Seerat Nabi (S.A.W.)

Who Is This Course For?

  • Kids who want to become Hafiz-e-Quran
  • Adults who want to memorize selected Surahs
  • Students who have completed Quran reading with Tajweed
  • Anyone with a goal to memorize the full Quran
  • Students in USA, UK, Canada, Australia and worldwide
